Bitcoin...

Bitcoin often described as a cryptocurrency – is a type of money that is completely virtual, like an online version of cash. You can use it to buy products and services, but not many shops accept Bitcoin and interestingly some countries have banned it altogether. But companies, however, are beginning to buy into its growing influence.

For example, 2 years ago in October, PayPal announced that it would be allowing its customers to buy and sell Bitcoin through PayPal.

Each Bitcoin is basically a computer file which is stored in a ‘digital wallet’ app on a smartphone or computer for example: Meta Mask.

People can send bitcoins and you can send Bitcoins to other people through your digital wallet.

So how do people get Bitcoin?

There is mainly three ways:

- You can buy Bitcoins with ‘real’ money

- You can sell things and let people pay you with Bitcoins

- Or they can be created using a computer
